Imam Maalik Biography

Full Name: Maalik bin Anas bin Maalik
Parents: Anas bin Abi Aans (Maalik)
Birth Date: 93 AH/712 CE MEDINA
Residence: MEDINA

Imam Maalik was taught by Muhammad Ibn Muslim Ibn Shihaab Zuhri, Aamir Ibn Abdullah Ibn Zuubayr, Zayd Ibn Aslam and so on. Imam Maalik taught a great number of students. Qaazi Ayaadh(R.A) has recorded over 1300 amongst this group were Imam Maalik's own teachers, scholars, peers and subordinates. Namely a few are Ibn Shihaab Zuhri, Yahyaa Ibn Saheed Al-Qaari, Yazeed Ibn Abdullah and so on.
