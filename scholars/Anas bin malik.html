Anas bin Malik Biography

Full Name: Anas bin Malik b. al-Nadr b. DamDam
Parents: Malik ibn Nadr
Birth Date: 10 BH/613 CE MEDINA
Residence MEDINA

Anas bin Malik was born a non-muslim. He accepted Islam along with his mother even though his father,Malik bin Nadr, refused to do so.
